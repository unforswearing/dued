Dued is an Alfred extension and system service for formatted dates.



dued, yeah!

To use dued, download whatever version will suit your needs and have fun fulfilling your hearts desires. Holler at me if you have questions, compliments, or complaints -- notes @ theforeverarrow.com.

dued, what?

dued started as a very simple way to get tomorrows date but it quickly expanded. Visit the dued Help page for a bit of documentation. You can also run the script and type help or dued help in the Alfred workflow and the help page will open in your default browser. The dued Alfred workflow and system service are written in AppleScript.

dued, more!!

I do not want to overload dued with options, but I am working on a few ideas. Check the To Do section of the Help page for info.
